About The Studio

We design spaces that feel composed, useful, and deeply tied to place.

Studio Yam is an architectural practice focused on refined residential, interior, and community projects. We believe strong architecture comes from disciplined editing: fewer gestures, better light, and details that support everyday life.

Our work is collaborative from the start. We bring clients, consultants, and builders into the conversation early so design decisions remain coherent through concept, documentation, and delivery.

What guides us

  • Context before style
  • Material honesty and longevity
  • Clear plans and generous circulation
  • Careful alignment between architecture and interiors
